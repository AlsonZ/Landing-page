FAQ

Answering your questions.

If you have any additional questions, dont be afraid to send us an email or call our team for a consultation.

Do you provide refunds?

Yes, please have a look at our refund policy.

Can i customize my trip and the locations i want to visit?

Yes, we provide customised services if no existing package suits your needs.

Are there any hidden costs?

There are no hidden fees within our services.

Do i need to be vaccinated for my trip?

You should always consult a travel health clinic or your doctor to determine whether or not the area you are traveling to requires a vaccine. We always inform you of this before your confirmed purchase of a travel package.

Can you help me get a visa?

Sadly we do not provide visa approval services.

What should i pack on my trip?

When you purchase a trip package, you will be supplied with a detailed analysis of what you should do and bring on your trip.

Are there any age restrictions?

Children under the age of 5 are not recommended to go on physically taxing adventures. Minors (under 18), require the prescense of a guardian/parent on any trip.
